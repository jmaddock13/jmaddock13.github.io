Basic to intermediate tricks to learn on skis

There are a wide variety of tricks to learn on skis. There are easy/begginer tricks and then comes the harder, more complicated tricks. In this list you will learn how to do easy and intermediate tricks.

180 on skis

  1. practice doing a 180 on flat ground{make sure to rotate your head}
  2. start going on small jumps and pop off of them to get maximum height with your jump
  3. try your first 180 on a jump. make sure to do all the steps

Safety grab

  1. practice being comfortable with jumping with a pop
  2. while in the air start to bring your knees up to your chest
  3. after being comfortable with it start to grab your ski under the binding
  4. use all these steps and you can do it!

180 safety grab

  1. after being able to complete the first two tricks start combining them
  2. start by doing a 180 and bring up your knees to your chest
  3. once you can do that add the grab and there you have it

Mute grab

  1. start by laying on you back on the snow and getting used to grabing your left ski
  2. then while on a jump try to grab your left ski mid-air
  3. once you can touch your ski, start getting more air and grab your left ski entirely

These are only a few of many many tricks you can learn If you can master all of them there may be a list for more advanced and complicated tricks that you can learn. Use this list to impress your friends and teach them how to hit a mute grab yourself!
